Our journey

An Extraordinary Journey of "Coffee.in"

Brewing the Tale of Success

2010 Early Inspiration

In 2010, a group of coffee enthusiast decided to take a significant step in their life. They felt that the world needed more places that served high-quality coffee with a variety of options. The idea to establish Coffee.in began to take shape in their mind.

2011 Exploring The Right Place

After a year filled with research and planning, they began their quest to find the perfect location for their coffee shop. Eventually, they discovered a charming corner in the city center that would become the home of Coffee.in. They believed that a strategic location was the key to their business's success.

2012 The First Build

In 2012, the construction of Coffee.in commenced. The team worked tirelessly to create a cozy and inviting interior design, crafting the perfect ambiance for customers to enjoy a delightful cup of coffee. They also began sourcing the finest coffee beans to be used in their products.

2013 Grand Opening

After a year of hard work, Coffee.in was finally ready to open its doors. On June 15, 2013, Coffee.in officially embarked on its business journey with a grand and festive opening. First-time customers were greeted with warm smiles and an enticing coffee menu.

2014 Rapid Growth

In no time, Coffee.in began gaining popularity among coffee enthusiasts. Their signature products like "Hot Sensation" and "Iced Bliss" became customer favorites. Within a year, they had successfully opened two new branches in nearby cities.

2016 Product Inovation

The team were never content with the status quo. In 2016, Coffee.in introduced a new category on their menu, "Powder Coffee." It was an innovative coffee powder blend that could be enjoyed in unique ways. This showcased Coffee.in's commitment to bringing innovation to the world of coffee.

2018 Awards and Recognition

Outstanding coffee quality and friendly customer service earned Coffee.in various awards and accolades. They received the "Best Coffee Quality Award" from the Local Coffee Association and were recognized as one of the "Best Coffeeshops" by a leading food magazine.

2020 National Expansion

In their quest to expand their reach, Coffee.in decided to go national. They opened new branches in major cities across the country, allowing more people to savor their premium coffee quality.

2022 Community Engagement

Throughout this remarkable journey, Coffee.in actively engaged with the local community. They organized various charity events and environmental campaigns to give back to the community that had supported them over the years.
